Risk Register (Short Version)

  • Claims risk (highest): methylene blue and kava both draw FDA/FTC attention and complicate paid-ad approval. Mitigation: the claims guardrail file, week-7 gate, and channel strategy that leans on email, organic, and the warm lists — not just paid social.
  • Key-person dependency: the store's credibility story runs through one formulator. Mitigation: the seal + Science page make the method the asset, and the masthead brand stands on its own.
  • Scope creep: twelve SKUs at launch is already ambitious. Rule: no thirteenth product, no fifth sector, no feature additions until the week-one scoreboard exists.
